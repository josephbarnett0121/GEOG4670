Statement of Problem and Level of Effort
Noise pollution is an ever growing source of pollution that effects our daily lives. It is often overlooked because it does not appear visibily and often its effects are not as visible either however, it can be very harmful. Noise pollution can cause many different effects including cardiovascular disease, hearing loss, sleep disruption, and chronic stress. As a result of these potential hazards, I am going to investigate its effects on the state of Virginia in this project.
